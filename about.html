About Me

Hi, I'm Rattikarn! I love coding and I live in Foxboro, MA

I am currently a first year MS in computer Science at Boston University Metropolitan College. I am originally from Thailand and have lived in MA for 7 years.

I graduated from Payap Universiy located in Chiang Mai,Thailand and have a degree of BA in English. I found my self fastinated with a computer, starting from installing a software for classworks, building a web blog "my space" with a basic html and css and even assemble and disassemble laptops and desktops when it needed to be repir and replace components.

I had worked as an English teacher for years before decided to go to US to join an aupair program. After I finished an aupair program, I realized that I wanted to start my journey of coding.

I enrolled some CS courses from Quincy College while working as a childcare provider along with volunteering as a instructor assistant for robotic class at Wellesley Library. I finally obtained a certificate in Computer Science with an honor dean list. My dream and my goal is to be a software developer.

I believe having a strong fundamental in computer science will help me landing my dream job in a future.
